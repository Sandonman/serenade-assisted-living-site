Dining at Serenade

Breakfast is made to order each day so residents can choose what they are in the mood for. Lunches and dinners are shared meals designed to bring residents together in a warm, social setting. Dietary needs and personal preferences are accommodated whenever possible.

Breakfast Menu Essentials

  • Eggs (scrambled, fried, or omelet)
  • Bacon
  • Sausage
  • Toast and English muffins
  • Pancakes
  • Waffles
  • French toast
  • Oatmeal
  • Cold cereal
  • Yogurt
  • Fresh fruit
