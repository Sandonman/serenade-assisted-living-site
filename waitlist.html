Waitlist Eligibility & Placement Disclaimer

Serenade Assisted Living is a private-pay only community. We do not accept Arizona Long-Term Care System (ALTCS).

  • We do not accept residents with colostomy bags.
  • We do not accept residents who require constant nursing supervision.
  • We do not accept residents who require IV use.
  • We are licensed for 10 residents.
  • Vacancies are infrequent, and the waitlist is used whenever an opening becomes available.
  • Waitlist placement order may be adjusted based on current vacancy needs and candidate availability, so in some cases a lower waitlist position may be contacted first.

Serenade Assisted Living provides a full range of care for residents living with dementia and Alzheimer’s.
